Transaction Analysis and Trial Balance

Walsh & Company, Electrical Contractors began operations on May 1. The following transactions occurred during the month of May:

    May
  1. Stockholders invested $50,000 in the business in exchange for common stock.
  2. Purchased equipment on account, $4,400.
  3. Returned $400 of equipment that was not satisfactory. The return reduced the amount owed to the supplier.
  4. Purchased supplies on account, $1060.
  5. Purchased a truck for $10,700. Walsh paid $5,700 cash and gave a note payable for the balance.


Required

a. Create journal entries and record the above transacns in T-accounts, and key entries with the numbers of the transactions.
